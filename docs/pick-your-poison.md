# Pick your poison!

## Mod Loaders

There are two main mod loaders for Gorilla Tag.

- [MelonLoader](https://melonwiki.xyz/#/) (more modern, recommended)
- [BepInEx](https://github.com/bepinex/bepinex) (legacy, not recommended unless you need mods that utilize it)
